Welcome to Diaphorikos!

Graph first order differential equations with Diaphorikos!

What is Diaphorikos?

Inspired by Desmos, Diaphorikos is an attempt at elegantly displaying first order ordinary differential equations without too much hassle.

How to use Diaphorikos?

Head over here to get started! Here is what is supported:

  • All elementary algebraic operations (+,-,*,/) are supported.
  • All polynomial and exponential functions are supported.
  • All primary and reciprocal trigonometric functions are supported.
  • All inverse trigonometric functions are supported.
  • All hyperbolic and reciprocal functions are supported.
  • All inverse hyperbolic functions are supported.
  • The natural log \ln is supported. Other logarithms are not.
  • No constants (e.g. Ï€ or e) are supported.
  • Variables other than x and y are not supported.
